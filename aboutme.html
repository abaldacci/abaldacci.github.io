I am a Computer Vision consultant at NATO CMRE in La Spezia. I also serve as CEO and Computer Vision engineer for my company Transform And Lighting S.r.l. Previously, I was a research fellow at the Visual Computing Lab (ISTI-CNR) in Pisa.

I obtained my PhD in Computer Engineering from the University of Pisa. During my studies, I worked on a broad range of topics regarding both Computer Graphics and Computer Vision. See my publications page for more info.

I love to work on a variety of topics in the Computer Vision and Computer Graphics fields. Check out my projects page for more details.
